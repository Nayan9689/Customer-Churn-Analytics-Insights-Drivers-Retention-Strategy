# Customer-Churn-Analytics-Insights-Drivers-Retention-Strategy
End‑to‑end Customer Churn Analysis using Python, SQL &amp; Power BI. Explored churn drivers like tenure, contract type &amp; charges, identified high‑risk segments, and delivered actionable retention strategies. Highlights early‑tenure churn, pricing sensitivity, and contract‑based risk patterns.
Customer Churn Analysis Using Python, SQL & Power BI
📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes customer churn for a subscription‑based service to understand why customers leave and how businesses can reduce churn using data‑driven insights. It combines Python, SQL, and Power BI to uncover behavioral patterns and highlight high‑risk customer segments.

🎯 Problem Statement
The goal was to identify the key drivers of churn and pinpoint customer groups most likely to leave. These insights help the business take targeted retention actions and improve long‑term customer value.

🛠️ Approach & Tools
Python (EDA & Cleaning)
Handled missing values and inconsistencies

Explored distributions and correlations

Focused on tenure, contract type, monthly charges, and service usage

SQL (Structured Querying)
Segmented customers by tenure and contract

Identified churn patterns across demographics and services

Power BI (Interactive Dashboard)
Built visualizations for churn trends

Highlighted high‑risk customer groups

Created actionable business insights

🔍 Key Insights
Month‑to‑month contract customers had the highest churn, indicating low commitment.

Customers in their first 0–24 months were most likely to leave, showing early‑stage disengagement.

High‑risk customers had higher monthly charges and shorter tenure, suggesting pricing sensitivity.

💼 Business Impact
Based on the findings, the following strategies were recommended:

Improve onboarding for new customers

Offer incentives for long‑term contracts

Target high‑risk customers with personalized retention campaigns

Adjust pricing or bundle services to reduce churn among sensitive segments

📈 Outcome
This project demonstrates how analytics can translate raw data into actionable business decisions that directly impact customer retention and revenue stability.
